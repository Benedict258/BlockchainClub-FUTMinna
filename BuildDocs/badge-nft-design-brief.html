Badge NFT Design Brief

BlockchainClub FUTMinna — 18 Non-Transferable SBT Badges

Design Specifications

Format: Square 1:1 ratio — SVG preferred, high-res PNG minimum 512×512px

Style: Dark theme — background #0D0014, accent #C084FC, border rgba(192,132,252,0.2)

Container: All 18 badges share a consistent hexagonal or rounded-square frame with a unified border treatment

Icon: Each badge has a unique central icon/symbol — no two badges should look alike

NFT: Minted as non-transferable SBTs on Sui blockchain

Color Usage: Colors below are the dominant accent for each badge. Designer has creative freedom within the dark theme palette.

Variants: Two states per badge — Locked (greyscale/muted) and Unlocked (full color).

Rarity tiers: Common (Achievement #1-8), Rare (#9-10), Epic (Challenge #11-16), Legendary (#17-18)
